About us

In the divine land of Gujarat, hospitality is not simply our culture but it runs in our blood.
This ideal has fuelled Prayasvin Patel's dream and transformed it to reality in the form of Madhubhan Resort and Spa. Prayasvin Patel is the Chairman and Managing Director of Elecon Engineering Co. Ltd. His pursuit to offer nothing but the best to his guests, has led to the creation of this ethno-contemporary resort that has turned out to be the best in its class.
The Madhubhan Resort & Spa project has been executed and promoted by Emtici Engineering, an Elecon Group Company headquartered in Vallabh Vidyanagar in Gujarat, India.

Madhubhan Resort & Spa is promoted by EMTICI Engineering, a marketing and service company involved in distributing a wide range of engineering products manufactured by Elecon Engineering Company Ltd., Eimco Elecon (India) Ltd., Power Build Ltd., Ringspann Elecon (I) Ltd., Belaz Technic Corporation and Sumitomo Heavy Industries.
